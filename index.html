What is Orton-Gillingham Tutoring?

Orton-Gillingham is a structured, science-based approach to reading instruction that has helped struggling readers for over 80 years. It is multisensory, explicit, and sequential—breaking reading into logical, manageable steps.

Rather than hoping children will “pick up” reading naturally, Orton-Gillingham tutors teach the rules explicitly using hands-on techniques that engage sight, sound, touch, and movement.

Who Benefits Most?

  • Dyslexia or reading difficulties
  • Slow progress in traditional classroom settings
  • Trouble sounding out words or spelling
  • Low confidence around reading and writing
