Supporting Students with Purpose

Orton-Gillingham Tutoring provides structured literacy instruction rooted in research, best practices, and a commitment to academic growth with a Christ-centered learning environment.

What is Orton-Gillingham Tutoring?

Orton-Gillingham is a structured, science-based approach to reading instruction that has helped struggling readers for over 80 years. It's a multi-sensory method that teaches students how letters and sounds work together—breaking reading down into manageable, logical steps.

Rather than hoping children will "pick up" reading naturally, Orton-Gillingham tutors teach the rules explicitly, using hands-on techniques that engage sight, sound, touch, and movement. This makes reading click for students who learn differently.

Who Benefits Most?

Orton-Gillingham is especially effective for students with:

Dyslexia or reading difficulties

Slow progress in traditional classroom settings

Trouble sounding out words or spelling

Low confidence around reading and writing

But it works for any student who needs structured, personalized reading support!
